Use an oil in the colder months to help seal in moisture.

Heavier, more occlusive oils can work especially well for drier skin types in winter.

Try a pure plant oil for the first step of your double cleanse.

Our organic oil will help break down and dissolve your makeup, so that you can go on to cleanse your skin.

Work a few drops of oil into your moisturizer.

WHAT ARE THE TIPS FOR APPLYING VEGETABLE OIL TO BODY, FACE AND HAIR?

For the body, massage your chest, arms, stomach and thighs zone by zone in circular movements. You will leave your skin illuminated, silky and firmed.
For the face, apply the oil or treatment in smoothing movements, starting from the center towards the contour of the face. The skin will be even softer and more elastic.
For the hair, apply the product directly by massaging it (do not forget the scalp). Then wrap them in a towel to leave on for 15 to 20 minutes to nourish and make them more shiny.
